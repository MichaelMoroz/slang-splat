## Gaussian Pipeline Optimization

Updated `shaders/renderer/gaussian_pipeline.slang` with two performance-focused changes:

- Increased raster workgroup and shared cache sizing to `16x16` (`256` lanes/entries) by changing:
  - `RASTER_BATCH` from `64` to `256`
  - `csRasterize` from `[numthreads(8, 8, 1)]` to `[numthreads(16, 16, 1)]`
  - lane index from `y * 8 + x` to `y * 16 + x`
- Switched non-positional cached data in rasterization from `float4` to `half4`:
  - `gsInvScale`, `gsQuat`, `gsColorAlpha`
  - Converted back to `float` at use sites for math operations
- Rewrote `csProjectAndBin` tile writeout to nested loops (`tileOffsetY` / `tileOffsetX`) with linear write cursor and guard counter, removing per-entry integer division/modulo from the old single-loop indexing path.
