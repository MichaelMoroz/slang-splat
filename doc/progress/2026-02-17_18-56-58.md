# 2026-02-17 18:56:58 - Fix COLMAP viewer prepass capacity feedback when stats are disabled

## Problem
- COLMAP viewer rendering uses `render_to_texture(..., read_stats=False)` for main/debug paths.
- Internal prepass capacity growth relied on delayed counter feedback (`_update_delayed_counter_stats`), but this was previously gated behind `read_stats=True`.
- Result: with stats disabled, overflow feedback was not consumed and list-capacity adaptation could stall, causing persistent capacity-limited behavior on heavy COLMAP scenes.

## Changes
- `src/renderer/gaussian_renderer.py`
  - `render_to_texture(...)` now always calls `_update_delayed_counter_stats()` after GPU completion.
  - `read_stats` now controls only returned stat visibility, not internal adaptation logic.
- `tests/test_renderer_pipeline.py`
  - Added `test_prepass_capacity_feedback_progresses_with_stats_disabled`.
  - Renders several frames with `read_stats=False` under forced prepass pressure and verifies capacity feedback/growth is still active.
