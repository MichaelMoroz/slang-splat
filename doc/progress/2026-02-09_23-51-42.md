# Stability/anisotropy adjustments after sampled5 feedback

## Changes
- Replaced silhouette tangent-frame generation with Frisvad ONB construction in `generate_silhouette_points_local5` to remove abrupt basis flips from branchy reference-axis switching.
- Updated legacy projection conic output to anisotropic estimate from projected principal-axis spans (`e0/e1/e2`) instead of isotropic circle conic.

## Why
- Sampled5 jitter was dominated by frame construction discontinuities under small camera motion.
- Legacy debug conic previously ignored anisotropy and always emitted circle conic.

## Validation
- Ran `pytest -q`: `11 passed`.
